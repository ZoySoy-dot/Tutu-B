About us

Welcome to STEM 11-B's Digital Magazine, "TUTU-B: Technology Unites! Transforming & Uncovering Breakthroughs."! Our class is one of De La Salle University laguna campus grade 11 STEM students that are driven by curiosity, innovation, and a passion for science and technology. As part of our academic journey, we created this platform to explore pressing global issues, highlight groundbreaking research, and advocate for sustainability and inclusivity in the field of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Through in-depth articles, Diligent researching, and collaborating with other strands, we aim to inspire change and encourage critical discussions on how science can shape our world. Join us as we'll uncover ideas and turn it into action!

Mission Statement

STEM 11-B commits to the goal of fostering curiosity, innovation, and awareness through scientific exploration and digital storytelling. Our mission is to provide an inclusive platform that highlights breakthroughs in STEM addressing real-world challenges and inspiring positive change. Through research, collaboration, and advocacy, we strive to bridge the gap between science and society, empowering individuals to think critically and act responsibly.

Vision Statement

We envision a future where STEM is accessible, inclusive, and transformative. By sharing knowledge and promoting sustainability, we aim to cultivate a community of forward-thinking individuals who use science and technology to build a better, more informed, and equitable world. Our vision is to inspire curiosity, spark innovation, and drive positive change through digital storytelling and scientific exploration.
