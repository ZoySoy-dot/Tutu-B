About us

Welcome to tutu-B: Technology Unites! Transforming & Uncovering Breakthroughs—the digital magazine of STEM 11-B from De La Salle University – Laguna Campus. We’re a group of Grade 11 STEM students who are curious, creative, and passionate about science and technology. This magazine is part of our academic journey, created to dive into important global issues, highlight innovative ideas, and promote sustainability and inclusivity in the world of STEM. With each article, we aim to share what we’ve learned, encourage meaningful conversations, and show how science and technology can help build a better future. We’ve also worked with students from other strands to bring fresh perspectives and new insights to our content. Thanks for joining us—let’s explore, discover, and make a difference together!

Our Mission

STEM 11-B is committed to cultivating curiosity, innovation, and awareness through the fusion of scientific exploration and digital storytelling. Our mission is to build an inclusive and dynamic platform that highlights significant breakthroughs in STEM—showcasing their relevance to real-world challenges and inspiring transformative change. Through rigorous research, collaborative efforts, and student-led advocacy, we strive to bridge the gap between science and society by making complex ideas more accessible, engaging, and impactful. By promoting critical thinking, creative problem-solving, and ethical responsibility, we empower individuals to not only understand scientific progress—but to actively shape a future driven by knowledge, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Vision

We envision a future where STEM is accessible, inclusive, and transformative—empowering individuals from all backgrounds to explore, learn, and innovate. By sharing knowledge and championing sustainability, we aspire to build a global community of forward-thinking individuals who leverage science and technology to confront pressing challenges and shape a more informed, equitable, and sustainable world. Through education, collaboration, and the power of digital storytelling, we aim to ignite curiosity, fuel creativity, and drive meaningful change. By making STEM both engaging and relatable, we strive to break down barriers, foster lifelong learning, and cultivate a generation of problem-solvers who use science not just for discovery, but for the advancement of society as a whole.
