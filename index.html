About us

Welcome to tutu-B: Technology Unites! Transforming & Uncovering Breakthroughs—the digital magazine of STEM 11-B from De La Salle University – Laguna Campus. We’re a group of Grade 11 STEM students who are curious, creative, and passionate about science and technology. This magazine is part of our academic journey, created to dive into important global issues, highlight innovative ideas, and promote sustainability and inclusivity in the world of STEM. With each article, we aim to share what we’ve learned, encourage meaningful conversations, and show how science and technology can help build a better future. We’ve also worked with students from other strands to bring fresh perspectives and new insights to our content. Thanks for joining us—let’s explore, discover, and make a difference together!

Mission Statement

STEM 11-B is dedicated to fostering curiosity, innovation, and awareness by integrating scientific exploration with digital storytelling. Our mission is to create an inclusive and engaging platform that showcases significant breakthroughs in STEM, emphasizing their impact on real-world challenges and inspiring meaningful change. Through rigorous research, collaborative efforts, and advocacy, we aim to bridge the gap between science and society by making complex concepts more accessible and relevant. By encouraging critical thinking, problem-solving, and ethical responsibility, we empower individuals to not only understand scientific advancements but also to actively contribute to a future driven by knowledge, innovation, and sustainability.

Vision Statement

We envision a future where STEM is accessible, inclusive, and transformative, empowering individuals from all backgrounds to explore, learn, and innovate. By sharing knowledge and advocating for sustainability, we strive to build a global community of forward-thinking individuals who harness the power of science and technology to address pressing challenges and shape a better, more informed, and equitable world. Through education, collaboration, and digital storytelling, we aim to inspire curiosity, spark creativity, and drive meaningful change. By making STEM engaging and relatable, we hope to break barriers, encourage lifelong learning, and cultivate a generation of problem-solvers who use science not just for discovery, but for the betterment of society.
