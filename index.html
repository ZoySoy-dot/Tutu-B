About us

Welcome to tutu-B: Transcending Universal Thresholds, Unlocking Breakthroughs — the official digital magazine of STEM 11-B from De La Salle University – Laguna Campus.

At tutu-B, we believe that science and technology are more than just academic disciplines — they are powerful forces for transformation, innovation, and social change. This digital magazine is a reflection of who we are: a group of Grade 11 STEM students united by our shared curiosity, creativity, and drive to explore the world through a scientific lens.

Our journey began with a simple goal: to create a platform that amplifies student voices and brings STEM topics to life in meaningful ways. As part of our academic project, tutu-B evolved into a space where we could research, write, design, and collaborate to share what we’ve learned — not just with our classmates, but with a broader audience of peers, educators, and lifelong learners.

Each article we publish is the product of careful research, critical thinking, and thoughtful collaboration. We tackle pressing global issues — from climate change and sustainability to emerging technologies, digital ethics, and inclusive innovation. We don’t just highlight advancements; we explore their impact on society and reflect on the responsibilities that come with scientific progress.

What makes tutu-B unique is the diversity of voices behind it. We've worked closely with students from other strands to bring fresh perspectives, bridge disciplines, and spark interdisciplinary conversations. Together, we aim to connect science with everyday life and make complex ideas more accessible, engaging, and relevant to our generation.

As you browse through our pages, we hope you find stories that challenge your thinking, inspire your curiosity, and encourage you to take action. Whether you’re a student, educator, or simply someone passionate about science and technology, tutu-B invites you to join us on this journey of discovery.

Thank you for being here. Let’s explore ideas, uncover breakthroughs, and build a better future — together.

Our Mission

STEM 11-B is committed to cultivating curiosity, innovation, and awareness through the fusion of scientific exploration and digital storytelling.

Our mission is to create an inclusive and dynamic platform where students can explore, question, and contribute to the world of science and technology. We believe that STEM is not just a field of study — it is a lens through which we can understand complex global challenges, find solutions, and imagine a better future. tutu-B was born from this belief, giving us a voice and a space to share what we’ve learned and what we care deeply about.

Through rigorous research, thoughtful writing, and collaborative teamwork, we highlight breakthrough ideas and emerging innovations that are shaping the world around us. From sustainability and climate change to digital ethics and medical advancements, we aim to present STEM not as something distant or difficult — but as something approachable, impactful, and deeply human.

We also recognize that the best solutions come from diverse minds working together. That’s why we collaborate with students from other academic strands to bring in fresh perspectives, challenge assumptions, and promote interdisciplinary thinking. Our goal is to bridge the gap between science and society — to make complex ideas not only understandable, but meaningful and relevant to real people.

At tutu-B, we champion critical thinking, creativity, and ethical responsibility. We believe that education should not just inform — it should empower. By sharing our stories, insights, and discoveries, we hope to inspire others to take action, ask questions, and become agents of change in their own communities.

Through every article and every collaboration, we are learning, growing, and striving to shape a future driven by knowledge, innovation, and a deep sense of purpose.

Our Vision

We envision a future where STEM is accessible, inclusive, and transformative—empowering individuals from all backgrounds to explore, learn, and innovate.

At tutu-B, we believe that science and technology should not be limited to textbooks or laboratories, but should be tools for social progress, creativity, and empowerment. Our vision is to help reshape the way STEM is perceived and experienced—breaking down barriers and opening doors for students, educators, and communities to connect through shared discovery.

By promoting sustainability, inclusivity, and global awareness, we aim to foster a community of forward-thinkers who use STEM to address the challenges that matter most—climate change, inequality, innovation gaps, and more. We see a future where scientific literacy is universal, where young minds are encouraged to question, explore, and build solutions, regardless of their background or identity.

Education is at the heart of this vision. Through collaboration, storytelling, and creative expression, we strive to make STEM content not only understandable but deeply engaging. We want learners to see themselves reflected in science, to feel curious instead of intimidated, and to recognize the real-world value of knowledge.

tutu-B serves as a living example of what students can achieve when given the freedom to explore and the tools to express. By making science and technology personal, purposeful, and human, we aim to spark a lifelong love for learning—and inspire a new generation of problem-solvers who use STEM not just for discovery, but to shape a more compassionate, informed, and sustainable world.

We don’t just imagine the future—we’re building it, one story at a time.

Our Publications

As you browse through our articles, we invite you to engage with the ideas, insights, and stories that we have shared. We hope that our work sparks conversations, challenges assumptions, and inspires you to think differently about the world around you. Thank you for joining us on this journey of discovery, exploration, and transformation.
